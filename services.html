What We Do

Done Right. Done Properly.

When you want a stump gone, you just want it done — and done properly. Old stumps can attract bugs, fungus, damage mowers, and even get in the way of landscaping projects. Allow us to give you a free quote, expert advice, and help you get rid of that pesky stump safely.

⚠
Call Before You Dig! Always dial 811 or visit missouri-811.org before any digging so underground utilities are safely marked. We can make the call for you — just let us know when scheduling.

If no available time fits your schedule, call us directly and we’ll accommodate you: (816) 237-8758

ⓘ  Price will vary if there is more than one stump. Please include how many stumps you need removed when scheduling.
